    Honeybee

    ⬤ Berechnung von Heiz- und Kühlspitzenlasten sowie stündliche Energiebedarfe zur Dimensionierung von HLK-Systemen. Simulation von Design-Tagen (99. Perzentil) unter Berücksichtigung von Gebäudehülle, Wärmequellen, Lüftung und internen Lasten.     ⬤ Calculation of heating and cooling peak loads and hourly energy demands for HVAC system sizing. Simulation of design days (99th percentile) accounting for building envelope, heat sources, ventilation, and internal gains. Quantification of passive strategies (shading, thermal mass, natural ventilation) impact on system size and operating costs. Prevention of oversizing through dynamic simulation – optimization of HVAC efficiency (COP) and lifecycle costs.
