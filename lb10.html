    ⬤ Psychrometric and bioclimatic diagrams (ASHRAE 55, Olgyay, Givoni) visualizing comfort zones and passive strategies. Adaptive comfort standards with configurable boundaries for different user groups (office, residential, healthcare). Quantitative output: comfort hours and exceedance frequencies. Design feedback: iterative optimization of solar gains, thermal mass, and ventilation strategies to maximize comfort.
