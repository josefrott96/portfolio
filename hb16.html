    ⬤ Identifikation optimaler Verschattungszonen durch Analyse saisonaler Energieflüsse (Solargewinne, Transmissionsverluste). Berechnung von Regionen wo Verschattung Kühlenergie reduziert OHNE Heizenergie negativ zu beeinflussen.     ⬤ Identification of optimal shading zones through analysis of seasonal energy flows (solar gains, transmission losses). Calculation of regions where shading reduces cooling energy WITHOUT negatively affecting heating energy. Seasonal differentiation: summer cooling load minimization vs. winter solar gain preservation. Window and facade-specific strategies (orientation, geometry, control) optimizing energy balance, comfort, and glare – holistic facade planning with measurable efficiency gains.
