    ⬤ I hold a Master's degree in Resource-Efficient and Sustainable Building at the Technical University of Munich (M.Sc., 2025). My architectural background is founded on studies at the Leopold Franzens University Innsbruck (B.Sc., 2022) and the University of Ferrara (2019). Currently, I live in Munich.

    My areas of focus are:
    ● Building in existing structures
    ● Climate-responsive building
    ● Computational Design

    During my studies, I contributed to various research projects at the Chair for Energy-Efficient and Sustainable Planning and Building under Prof. Dr.-Ing. Werner Lang (2023-2025) as well as at the Chair for Building Technology and Climate-Responsive Building under Prof. Thomas Auer (2021-2024).

    My interest in sustainable building was first sparked during my interior architecture studies at the Technical University of Rosenheim (B.A., 2020), where I served as team leader of the Solar Decathlon Europe 22.

    In the future, I would like to actively shape the building transition with my specialist knowledge.
