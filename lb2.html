    ⬤ Geometric visualization of sun path (sun-path diagrams) over full year with hourly resolution. Stereographic projection of sky hemisphere with azimuth and elevation angles. Real-time animations of solar movement (sunrise/sunset, noon) for any day. Combination with 3D building models calculating shading patterns and insolation periods. Direct application for climate-responsive facade orientation, window placement, and louver strategy – foundation for passive solar energy utilization and daylighting design.
